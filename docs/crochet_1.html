Beautiful Crochet Blanket

Project Description

This crochet blanket was inspired by the cozy textures of winter. It's made with a soft, chunky yarn that ensures warmth and comfort. The design features a combination of simple stitches that create a beautiful, textured pattern.

This blanket measures approximately 60 x 70 inches, making it perfect for snuggling on the couch. The project took about 20 hours to complete, and it was a joy to see the pattern come together. This blanket makes a great gift or a lovely addition to your home decor.

Materials Used

  • Bulky weight yarn (10 skeins)
  • Size N/15 (10.0 mm) crochet hook
  • Scissors
  • Tapestry needle
